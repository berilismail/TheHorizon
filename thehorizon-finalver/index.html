About Us

Founded in 1987, The Horizon Museum began as a modest initiative with a dream to bring art and history closer to our community. What started with a single exhibition in a small gallery has blossomed into a premier cultural institution renowned for its expansive collections and innovative programming. Over the years, we have expanded our facilities, enriched our collections, and forged valuable partnerships, establishing ourselves as a beacon of cultural and educational enrichment in the region.

Our Mission

At The Horizon Museum, our mission is to illuminate the stories of humanity through captivating exhibitions and educational experiences. We are dedicated to preserving the richness of our cultural heritage and inspiring future generations. By curating a diverse array of art and artifacts, we strive to foster a deeper understanding and appreciation of the world’s shared history and creativity.
